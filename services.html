Our Services

Shalom Home Care offers a comprehensive suite of services to support elderly clients and help them continue living independently in their own homes. Our dedicated caregivers are trained to deliver high-quality care tailored to each client's individual needs.

  • Personal Care Assistance

    Our trained caregivers provide help with daily living activities such as bathing, grooming, dressing, and medication management. They ensure clients maintain good hygiene and personal care, enhancing their quality of life.

  • Medication Management

    We partner with healthcare providers to coordinate skilled nursing care, physical therapy, occupational therapy, and other clinical services as required. Our approach ensures both safety and compliance with medication schedules.

  • Meal Preparation

    Caregivers meticulously plan, shop for, and prepare nutritious meals tailored to the client's dietary needs and preferences, ensuring that clients have easy access to healthy food options that respect their personal tastes.

  • Light Housekeeping

    Caregivers assist with light housekeeping tasks such as laundry, cleaning, organizing, and running errands. This support helps clients keep their homes tidy and comfortable, promoting a pleasant living environment.

  • Companion Care

    Our caregivers provide friendly conversation, engage clients in activities, and offer emotional support. This addresses the risk of social isolation that many elderly individuals face, helping to enhance their overall well-being.

  • Transportation

    Shalom Home Care provides safe and reliable transportation to medical appointments, social activities, religious services, and other destinations as needed, helping clients remain active and engaged in their community.
