Our Welcoming Message

Shalom Home Care is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing high-quality, compassionate care for elderly individuals in the community. Our mission is to enable seniors to live independently and with dignity in their own homes for as long as possible. Shalom Home Care offers a range of services including personal care assistance, light housekeeping, meal preparation, transportation, and companionship.
